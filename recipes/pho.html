Pho!

Steps

Quick Boil- Remove imipurities from beef within a 5 minute boil. it's the path to a beautiful clear soup.
Scum - be amazed at all the icky stuff that comes out;
Wash the bones to get all the icky scum off;
Simmer for 3 hours - bones, beef water, onion, ginger and spices (cinnamon,cardamom ,coriander, star anise)
Remove brisket - some is used for Pho topping
Simmer 40 minutes further with just bones;
strain ;then
Ladle into bowls

Ingredients

  • cinnamon
  • star anise
  • 3 cloves
  • corriander seeds

Description

A delicious Viet soup! :)
